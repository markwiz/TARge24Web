About Us: The Pedal Faster Legacy

At Pedal Faster, our journey began in the most unconventional way possible: a group of visionaries who saw potential where others saw “missing property.” In an era where bicycles mysteriously changed owners overnight, we realized the world needed a solution—a trusted name in bicycle redistribution.

With unparalleled expertise in locating, refurbishing, and occasionally explaining why a customer’s new purchase looked vaguely familiar, we built Pedal Faster into a beacon of innovation. Our repainting techniques set industry standards, ensuring every bike found its unique identity (and stayed that way).

When public scrutiny increased (some say unfairly), we pivoted to full legitimacy. Today, Pedal Faster is a leading name in the cycling world, providing top-quality bikes and accessories with an ironclad “No Questions Asked” policy. We pride ourselves on our rich history, our resilience, and our ability to pedal out of any tight situation—literally and metaphorically.
